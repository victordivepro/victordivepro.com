FAQ

Can I dive with no experience?

Yes. The Discovery Dive is made for first-timers: full briefing on the surface, then a calm guided dive with an instructor right next to you.

Do I need to be a good swimmer?

Usually no — if you feel comfortable in the water and have no medical restrictions, the instructor supports you throughout the dive.

Where do dives take place?

Hon Mun Marine Protected Area and nearby sites — a short boat ride from Nha Trang. The exact spot depends on the day's conditions.

What's included?

Hotel transfer (within the city), full equipment, briefing, instructor guidance, light breakfast and lunch on the boat. Photo/video available on request.

How do I book?

Message us on WhatsApp or Telegram: your date, number of guests, experience and hotel. We reply within an hour.
